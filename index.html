About Mill Bakery

A neighborhood bakery with warm, familiar favorites

Mill Bakery is a local spot serving fresh Mexican pastries, classic pan dulce, hot coffee, and satisfying breakfast burritos. Whether you are picking up a quick morning bite or bringing home a box of pastries, the bakery is made to feel easy, welcoming, and close to home.
